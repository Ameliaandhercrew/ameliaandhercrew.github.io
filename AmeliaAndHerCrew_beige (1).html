About Us

Welcome to Amelia and Her Crew, a family-run Labrador breeding program based in Dallas, Texas. Our beloved dam Amelia Under Kodas Eye brings courage and grace to our line, while our distinguished sire Cotton (Enchanted Oasis Rio Blanco of Yellow Rose MH) offers a calm disposition and strong working drive. We are passionate about raising healthy, happy Labradors that are ready for adventures with their new families.

Meet the Parents

Amelia Under Kodas Eye

Our fearless dam, named after the pioneering aviator Amelia Earhart. She is affectionate, intelligent, and has a strong pedigree of health and temperament.

Enchanted Oasis Rio Blanco of Yellow Rose MH (“Cotton”)

Cotton, our distinguished sire, brings hunting titles and a gentle demeanor to the mix. His calm disposition and strong working drive make him the perfect partner for Amelia.

Adoption Process & Payment

To reserve a puppy from this litter, we require a deposit. Once you express interest, we’ll provide further details on deposit amounts and payment schedules.

All puppies will come with up-to-date vaccinations, de-worming, and a microchip. Before going home, each puppy will receive a health check from our veterinarian.

Purchase Agreement & Health Guarantee

We’ve developed a comprehensive Puppy Purchase Agreement and Health Guarantee to protect both buyer and puppy. The contract covers deposit terms, health guarantees, spay/neuter requirements, and return policies. Please review our contract carefully and contact us with any questions.

You can obtain a copy of our contract by reaching out through the contact form or email below.
